  • How do I play?

    You can find a detailed guide on how to play on the homepage.

  • Does buying more than one ticket improve my chance of winning?

    Yes, it does. Each ticket you buy is displayed as a different entry and so, improves your chance of winning.

  • Is there a limit to the amount of tickets I can buy per game?

    You can buy up to {{ game.max_user_tickets_per_game }} tickets every game.

  • How are winners picked?

    The Win NGNT smart contract (what's a smart contract?) picks a ticket at random and sends their winnings to the address they used to buy their ticket.

  • How long does it take for winners to receive payment?

    Payment is made immediately a winner is chosen.

  • Do I need to create an account on this site?

    No, you don’t. All you need is a cryptocurrency wallet to store your NGNT. We recommend Trust Wallet for mobile devices and MetaMask for desktop browsers.

  • Can I get a refund on a ticket I purchased?

    No. Transactions already on the blockchain can’t be reversed.

  • What is NGNT?

    NGNT is a token that’s fiat-backed with real Naira held in licensed financial institutions (banks). Unlike most volatile digital currencies, NGNT is pegged 1:1 against the Naira. This means that for every NGNT out there on the network, there is an equivalent Naira amount held securely in bank accounts. You can learn more about it at ngnt.org.

  • How to redeem NGNT for Naira?

    In order to redeem your NGNT for Naira, you will need to send it to a wallet that supports withdrawals of Naira from NGNT. Such a wallet will collect your NGNT and send Naira to your chosen bank account.

  • Do I need to be a cryptocurrency expert to play?

    No, you don’t. The steps involved in playing are straightforward.

  • Are there any hidden charges?

    No.
